Hotels & Other Accommodation

Sri Lanka has an assortment of accommodation options.Colombo features not only a host of modern five-star hotels but also iconic colonial-era hotels with the charm and a bygone era. The coastal areas, especially the west and south, have innumerable resort hotels, where package tourists mostly stay.Hill country towns such as Kandy, Nuwara Eliya and Bandarawela feature colonial era hotels, and for those who venture farther afield, perhaps to indulge in adventure sports, there are beautifully converted colonial homes, tea and rubber plantation, buildings, jungle, tree houses and eco-lodges as well as camping include our travel plan.
